Merits of Using Macros for an Interactive Form


Automation: Macros allow you to automate the data entry process, reducing manual effort and potential errors. Once the macro is set up, users can simply click a button to perform various actions, such as submitting the form or clearing form fields.

Customization: Macros provide flexibility to customize the form's behavior and validation rules based on specific requirements. You can add additional features like data formatting, calculations, or conditional formatting to enhance the form's functionality.

Enhanced User Experience: Macros enable the addition of confirmatory buttons and dynamic responses, improving the user interface and interactivity. Users can receive instant feedback, error messages, or success notifications, making the form more user-friendly.
